Mercedes-Benz

Mercedes-Benz is a German global automobile marque and a division of Daimler AG. Mercedes-Benz is known for luxury vehicles, vans, trucks, buses, coaches and ambulances. The headquaters is in Stuttgart, Baden-Wurttemberg. The name first appeared in 1926 under Diamer-Benz. Mercedes-Benz combines luxury with performance across the full line of models including luxury sedans, SUVs, couples, roadsters, convertibles & more.
